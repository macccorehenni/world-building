#MagicTerm #FeyMagic #Condition

While _Fey-blooded_ often is used to describe all who can wield Fey magics, it strictly refers to those who can wield [[Fey magic]], but not those who can purify the [[Feybraid]] (that is, the [[Fey-touched]]). While anyone in the Multi-world could be Fey-blooded only those who have been exposed to an area where the Feyveil is strong will know if this is the case.
